About

The Podcast


Many physicians feel overwhelmed by private practice and the business of medicine. This makes sense. Physicians take a 10+ year path of clinical education and training to become the best physicians and surgeons. We have an idea of how we want to practice before we start, and we are just waiting to finish our training so that we can achieve our dream practice setting. This idea keeps us going through all the ups and downs of training; we sacrifice a great deal but we are excited to have the honor of being physicians who are skilled at helping our patients.

That passion for clinical care never leaves us.

But along the way to becoming practicing physicians, we don’t learn about our job opportunities. We don’t understand how we can start our own practices. We don’t learn the business acumen needed to thrive in private practice. We don’t learn about the business of medicine. We don’t set ourselves up for success with a mentor. We really don’t know how to survive in today’s medical landscape and to be the physician leaders needed in our communities. We get burnt out trying to keep up.

Unstoppable Docs was created to empower physician and to learn from physician leaders who are, in a word, unstoppable. What does that mean? We are the 45% of physicians in the USA who are owners of their practices, whether solo or in a group. Unstoppable Docs are serving their patients in innovative ways. They are navigating their businesses and often diving into uncharted entrepreneurial territories, as they champion the business of medicine and create private practices that allow them to take back medicine and deliver excellent patient care on their own terms.

Let’s empower ourselves to take on the world of private practice and all things related to the business of medicine. Tune in for our next episode, and if you have someone that fits the bill, email us so we can connect.
